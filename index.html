Dark Places Anchorages

Sharon thought she left the shadows behind after a weekend retreat at a remote cabin, but something came home with her. As the darkness deepens, the quiet safety of her suburban neighborhood transforms into a nightmarish maze of paranoia, haunting apparitions, and violent encounters. With her friends experiencing horrors of their own and the boundaries of reality unraveling, Sharon must confront the chilling truth: sometimes, the scariest places aren't the ones you visit but the ones you unknowingly bring with you.

Will Sharon uncover the sinister force that has anchored itself to her life before it's too late? Or will the shadows claim her as their next victim?

Step into the terror and discover what lurks in Dark Places.
